Meet the Owner


Stacey, owner of Honest Burgers, grew up on a farm in Massachusetts. Growing up on a farm, and knowing where her food was coming from, inspired Stacey to open Honest Burgers.


Honest Burgers provides a unique "Farm to Table" dining experience for guests. As you dine, you can learn about the local farms Stacey partners with to provide the highest quality grass fed beef and chicken.

Stacey is especially interested in food sustainability
